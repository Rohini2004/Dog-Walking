Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our dog walking service

Our standard walks are 30 minutes, but we offer flexible durations from 20 to 60 minutes. We'll customize the walk length based on your dog's energy level, age, and needs.

We currently service all major neighborhoods within the city limits. Enter your zip code during booking to check if we're available in your area.

Through our mobile app, you'll receive real-time GPS tracking, photo updates, and a detailed report after each walk, including route, duration, and bathroom breaks.

We'll notify you of any weather concerns and can either reschedule the walk or adjust the duration. For light rain, we provide towel-drying service after the walk.
